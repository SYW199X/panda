Organisation

The basic operational combat formation of the PLAGF is the corps-sized Group Army (GA) which commands a series of brigades both manoeuvre and support. GAs are also supported by higher-echelon subordinate specialist brigades that provide more advanced support such as strategic reconnaissance, signals, combat engineering, and electronic warfare capabilities among others. GAs are assigned to a theatre command which are geo-strategic partitions that specialise in planning and training against threats in their specific axis. For example, the Eastern Theatre Command specialises in the Taiwan contingency as well as potential confrontations with Japan and USPACOM. During peacetime, the thirteen GAs are distributed quite evenly across the five theatre commands but can be concentrated flexibly during wartime.
