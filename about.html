About Us

The huge changes to the PLA have multiple implications. One, the world is increasingly curious about the PLA as power begets attention. Two, the PLA is changing rapidly and the few who could be bothered to put in the effort to understand the PLA in the first place quickly find their hard-earned understanding outdated. Three, owing to point one, many are finding out how difficult it is to get accurate information about the PLA due to the language barrier and the PLA's inherent opaqueness. These factors coalesce into sufficient cause for the establishment of this website which aims to provide accessible and comprehensible up-to-date information about the PLA.

This website was built, populated, and is maintained by volunteers. If you would like to contact us, please feel free to complete and submit the form below.
