About Us

Carnivorous Panda is a repository for the knowledge accumulated by a dedicated group of mil-watchers specialising in PLA developments. The recent transformations in the PLA have exceeded all expectations and keeping up with developments for the casual enthusiast is exceedingly difficult. This website thus collates some of the best open-source information regarding the PLA on this side of the language barrier into an easily navigable and digestible site for all.

This website was built, populated, and is maintained by volunteers. If you would like to contact us, please feel free to do so via the form below.
