About Us

The Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) is the armed forces of the People's Republic of China. It is comprised of five service branches; the Ground Force, Navy, Air Force, Rocket Force, and Strategic Support Force, and totals two million active personnel. In recent decades, the PLA has seen tremendous modernisation and has enjoyed a vast increase in combat potential despite being more than halved in size from over four million since 1980.

The immense changes to the PLA has multiple implications. One, the world is increasingly curious about the PLA as power begets attention, and the PLA and the China as a whole is becoming more powerful. Two, the PLA is changing rapidly and the few who could be bothered to put in the effort to understand the PLA in the first place quickly find their hard-earned understanding outdated. Three, owing to point one, many are finding out how difficult it is to get accurate information about the PLA due to the language barrier and the PLA's inherent opaqueness. These factors coalesce into sufficient cause for the establishment of this website which aims to provide accessible and comprehensible up-to-date information about the PLA.
