The Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) is the armed forces of the People's Republic of China. It is comprised of five service branches; the Ground Force, Navy, Air Force, Rocket Force, and Strategic Support Force, and totals two million active personnel. In recent decades, the PLA has seen tremendous modernisation and has enjoyed a vast increase in combat potential despite being more than halved in size from over four million in 1980.
